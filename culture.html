Cultural Variables

Being Indian, I have had a very different upbringing compared to most Americans, as my parents are much more focused on my academics and studies, over sports and social life.

Culture in my life

My parents were the first in my extended family to move to the US, and I was the first person in my entire family to be entirely educated in the US. This puts a lot of pressure on me, to get into a good college and become successful enough to support my parents.

While growing up, I haven't had an issue with being a minority, and have rather made it part of me. Joking around with my culture has become a part of me, and being in America, I have never had many issues with being prejudiced against.
