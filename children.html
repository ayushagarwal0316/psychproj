Children

Once I have been married, I would like to start a family. Not right away, but after buying my own property and becoming stable. Having children is obviously time consuming and expensive, and it would ultimately be up to my wife as to when we'd have kids and how many. Apart from children, I plan on getting a pet, mainly a dog, which is equally time consuming as a child, as it would introduce me to the responsibilities of having a life fully dependent on you.
