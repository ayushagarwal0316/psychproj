    The Four Parenting Styles

  • Authoritarian parents are those who are very strict and controlling of the child. They set strongly enforced rules and punish the children accordingly. These parents would be the type to discourage independent thinking and force the children to do as the parents tell them.
  • Authoritative parents are more open than Authoritarian parents, and less restrictive and demanding. They are more supportve of their children and want them to be socially responsible but also active.
