Genetic Makeup

One's genetic makeup consists of traits one inherits from their parents.

Parental Traits to Self

As DNA is passed down from the parents to the child, the child takes upon some of the parents traits. For example, my mother and I have more oval shaped eyes, whereas my father and sister have round eyes.

Dominant and Recessive Traits

Dominant traits are those which tend to be more common and beneficial in life. A dominant trait which I inherited is having a detached earlobe, which my mother carries but not my father, who has a recessive trait.

Influence of Genetic Traits

My parents have given me many traits which have affected me greatly.

Sex Chromosomes

I am a male, so my sex chromosomes are XX

Teratogens

As my mother did not smoke or drink alcohol, no toxins were put into her body while I was in her womb and I was born as a healthy infant.
